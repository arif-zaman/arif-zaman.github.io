Research

Our research interests span multiple areas, including high-performance systems and networking, data-intensive computing, and systems software. We conduct research on performance modeling, optimization, and anomaly detection in these areas. Specifically, we have been developing several transfer optimization algorithms that outperform existing solutions by up to an order of magnitude. Concurrently, we have developed network measurements and performance anomaly detection algorithms to complement the data transfer optimization process.

You can find the full list of our publications in Google Scholar.
